About MLK40

Forty years later we gather to honor Dr. King's vision and advance the next 40 years of leadership and opportunity. MLK40 unites visionaries, influencers, and students for a day of learning, certification, and investment in the creative economy. The initiative also supports a $1,000,000 infusion to fund scholarships, NIL programs, coursework, and institutional projects.

Program — 3-module Mini-Course

Attendees receive the MLK40 mini-course certification through three focused modules.

1. The Making of the MLK Holiday (1968—1986)

The producers of this 40th anniversary are the original producers of the first three years of the Federal Holiday in Memphis, Tennessee beginning in 1989, from the Orpheum Theatre. The presentation included the 1992, Opening Tribute for the National Civil Rights Museum. The tributes featured Martin Luther King, III, Rev. Dr. E.V. Hill, Rev. Clifton Davis, and other notables. Since then, the producers have gone on to producing national tributes across the nation including over 10 tributes at the MLK Memorial in collaboration with the MLK Memorial Foundation.

Learn More

2. The Black Tax: The Cost of Being Black in America

A frank look at economic, social, and institutional costs — and strategies for optimizing individual and community value.

Learn More

3. Our Move Now: Careers, Wealth, Institutions & Programs

Practical paths: careers, wealth creation, building institutions and programs that last for future generations.

Rev. Dr. Stephon Ferguson (on the left) is the only licensed person by the King Family to do the speeches of Rev. Dr. King. Rev. Arthur L. Hunt, Jr., is the Publisher of the first and only Bible honoring the genesis of King's Calling.

The Bible was dedicated on the 50th Anniversary of Rev. King’s final prophecy and ultimate assassination (1968—2018) on April 3-4, 2018, at the MLK Memorial in DC on the National Mall. Hosted in part by DC Mayor Muriel Bowser, a delegation of dignitaries honored the work of Rev. Dr. King as to the genesis of his calling as a Biblical Rights Leader.
